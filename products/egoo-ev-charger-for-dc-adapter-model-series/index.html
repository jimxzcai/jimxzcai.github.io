Why is it necessary for CCS2 to GB/T adapters to have built-in two 18650 batteries, while GB/T to CCS2 adapters do not require them?

  • The built-in batteries in the CCS2 to GB/T adapter are solely utilized to power the adapter during software upgrades.

Can I use your DC EV Charging Adapter in our commercial parking lots with different electric vehicle models?

  • Absolutely. Our adapter is designed with versatility in mind, supporting both GB/T CCS2 and CCS2-GB/T options. This means it’s compatible with a wide range of electric vehicles commonly found in commercial settings, ensuring your fleet or visitors can charge without any compatibility issues.

How does your adapter ensure safety during the charging process, especially in public charging stations where multiple users may access it?

  • Safety is paramount in our design. Our adapter comes equipped with Over Temperature Protection and Ground Protection features, enhancing safety during operation. Additionally, it complies with Standard EN IEC 62196, ensuring quality and reliability in every charging session, even in high-traffic environments.

What measures are in place to protect the adapter from damage or wear in outdoor charging stations exposed to various weather conditions?
